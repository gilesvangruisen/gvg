Thready for iPhone

I worked with another designer and personally led development of Thready for iPhone. Thready is a private discussion platform that lets small groups communicate with ease. I helped architect and implement a framework as well as a thin caching layer to interact with the Node server. Combining this with the MVVM powered card system (h/t ReactiveCocoa) resulted in a robust, snappy communication app.

Fetchnotes for iPhone

I helped design and build Fetchnotes for iPhone, a dead simple note-taking app that uses hashtags for organization and @tags for sharing. Because it's a hybrid app (built on Trigger.io), a lot of my work included writing modules to expose native iOS APIs to the AngularJS app in order to provide additional functionality and an overall better experience for our users.

Peddler Co.

I designed and prototyped a mobile e-commerce solution with a focus on fashion and apparel. Peddler lets users browse apparel and accessories, learning each person's taste from a stream of likes and dislikes. I learned a lot about app architecture and custom UI while building the native iOS interface to a Rails-powered backend.
